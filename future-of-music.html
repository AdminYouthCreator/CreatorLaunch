Our Partner: Future of Music

Empowering youth through music and business education.

What is Future of Music?

Future of Music is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering youth through music. They provide hands-on training in music production, DJing, and the fundamentals of the music business. Their goal is to inspire the next generation to explore their talents and pursue successful careers in the music industry.

Our Partnership

CreatorLaunch is proud to partner with Future of Music to expand opportunities for young entrepreneurs. As both organizations share a commitment to youth empowerment, our collaboration allows us to provide a unique intersection of creative and business education.

Through this partnership, Future of Music leverages CreatorLaunch's resources to offer students the chance to turn their musical and creative talents into real-world business ventures. This synergy is particularly strong given that Aereon Robinson, Co-Founder and Executive Manager of CreatorLaunch, is also the founder and operator of Future of Music, ensuring a deeply aligned vision and seamless collaboration.
