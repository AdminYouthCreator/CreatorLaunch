Workshop Instructor

Note: This is a volunteer position with the potential for a paid stipend ($20/hr), pending funding.

Lead a cohort of 4-8 students through our 6-week curriculum. You will be the primary mentor and guide, helping them turn their ideas into real, operating businesses.

Responsibilities:

  • Lead engaging weekly workshop sessions.
  • Deliver curriculum on topics like branding, marketing, and business finance.
  • Provide one-on-one mentorship and guidance to students.
  • Foster a positive, collaborative, and inspiring classroom environment.

Qualifications:

  • Experience in teaching, mentorship, or youth development.
  • Knowledge in one or more areas of business (entrepreneurship, marketing, etc.).
  • Excellent communication and facilitation skills.
  • A passion for empowering young people.
