Qwentin Blassingame

Founder & Executive Director

Driven by a profound passion for youth empowerment and the transformative potential of technology, Qwentin founded CreatorLaunch. His vision was to create an accessible, risk-free ecosystem where every young person, regardless of their background, could gain the tools and confidence needed to bring their creative and business ideas to life.

Qwentin leads the strategic direction of CreatorLaunch, ensuring our programs remain true to their mission of providing comprehensive, hands-on entrepreneurship education through real-world experience.
