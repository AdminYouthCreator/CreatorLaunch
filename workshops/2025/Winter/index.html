The Journey

Visually showcasing the 6-week curriculum as a series of exciting steps.

Week 1: Find Your Big Idea & Build Your Brand.

Kick off the workshop by discovering your unique business idea and establishing your brand's identity.

Week 3: Launch Your Own Shopify Store.

Get hands-on building your e-commerce presence and setting up your online storefront.

Week 4: Create Your First Marketing Campaign.

Learn strategies to reach your audience and design compelling campaigns to promote your business.

Week 6: Pitch Your Business to Community Leaders.

Present your completed business plan and online store to local entrepreneurs and mentors.
